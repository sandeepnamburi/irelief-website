iRelief Ops

Often times, natural disaster teams are remote, deplete of resources, or simply busy. iRelief Ops adds to the peer to peer part of aid. Simply hold hold your our S.O.S. button in the app for 5 seconds and your location, phone battery, and predicted conflict will be sent to people near you as well as goverment-aided disaster rescue team. We preserve the humanity in survival by connecting communities to more support emotionally and accessibly.

iRelief Aid

During times of need, there is no Sunday Walmart shopping or casual Craigslist searches. iRelief Aid provides immediate access to an automatically updated inventory of donations provided by people like you. People can also request certain essentials and donors can list their products and location for pickup. iRelief Aid provides a seamless yet unforgettable transaction of the meaning of a connected world.

Riley

Riley is an artificially intelligent hurricane relief bot for all your relief needs. Riley will help you with everything from a hug to an emergency food scarcity situation. Riley has the ability to quickly direct you to hundreds of resources both on and off the iRelief platform. It also hasd the ability to notify authorities for dire situations. Riley is available on Messenger and is available 24/7.
WARNING: DO NOT USE RILEY FOR EXPERIMENTAL PURPOSES.
